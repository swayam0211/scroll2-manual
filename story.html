The Omen

The Omen

Before the dragons returned,

the sky forgot its color.

For a hundred years, the mountains slept beneath ash,

and the sea held its breath.

The elders spoke of wings made of storm

and eyes that burned like fallen stars.

But no one believed them.

Until the wind changed.

The Warrior

The Warrior Without a Name

He walked where no shadow dared to follow.

Bound by an oath signed in blood and steel.

A ghost in the mist, a blade in the dark.

The Descent

The First Descent

When the sky split open,

it didn't rain water.

It rained fire and scale.

The first shadow to fall was the largest.

The Clash

Fire and Steel

Steel tempered in the heart of a dying star.

Fire that burns even the memory of cold.

The song of the blade is the only prayer left.

The Truth

The Truth Beneath the Flame

The flame doesn't just destroy.

It reveals what remains.

In the ashes of fear, we found our strength.

The Breaking

The Breaking of Fear

Fear is the heaviest chain.

Break it, and you will finally fly.

The dawn is coming, even if the sun is gone.

The Centerpiece

The Ember Pact

A promise made in the silence between heartbeats.

Ancient and unbreakable.

Blood for fire. Soul for light.

The Finality

The Sky Remembers

When the storm fades,

the sky remains.

Forever watching. Forever silent.

The chronicles end, but the myth begins.
